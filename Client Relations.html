Principles Guiding our Service Provision:

This service charter has been developed by the Management and Staff of Chriwah Hospital as a statement of our commitment in health service delivery to the people of Kasarani and beyond. The service charter spells out the quality and standard of service that we commit to offer all our clients. It highlights our responsibility as healthcare provider as well your rights and responsibilities as a client receiving Healthcare in our facility.

As a hospital, we are committed to serve our clients in line with the provisions of the Constitution of Kenya on rights of clients seeking health services in Kenya. This include:

  • The right to the highest attainable standard of health – Article 43(1); The right to life;
  • The right to emergency medical treatment – Article 43(2);
  • The right to the protection of your privacy and confidentiality –Article 31.
