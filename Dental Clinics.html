Dental Clinics

Chriwah Hospital Dental clinics aim to provide the very best in dental health care services. Our highly team of dentists, dental specialists and oral hygiene care providers offer a full range of dental treatment using the latest technologies. Our dental professionals receive continuous skills training from international and resident experts to ensure that our clients receive world class services. With state-of-the-art dental equipment (including safe digital x-rays, intra-oral photography), up-to-date techniques (such as root canal treatments, implants, ceramic crowns) and modern materials, we are the dental centres of excellence for the East African region. For our client's convenience, the AKUH,N dental clinics are located at various locations in East Africa including Nairobi, Arusha and Kampala, and we are continuously expanding. Clients can call the clinics directly to schedule appointments. Welcome to AKUH Dental Clinics, where we go the extra mile to make you smile!.

​The Chriwah Hospital, Nairobi offers comprehensive dental services provided by a highly trained and experienced dental team.
Some of the services we offer include:
  • Aesthetic Dentistry
  • Prosthetics (crowns, bridges & dentures)
  • Restorative Dentistry
  • Paediatric Dentistry
  • Oral Surgery
