Heart and Cancer Centre

A first of its kind in East Afric​​a, the centre provides high quality care and treatment based on international standards for full spectrum of heart and cancer conditions.

The population of sub-saharan Afric​a c​an now access specialised care with confidence and without having to travel abroad for treatment.

The centre located at the Jimmy Sayani Building consists of five operating theatres, twenty-three intensive care beds (including a cardiotherapic intensive care unit) and separate coronary intensive care u​​​nit, neonatal intensive care unit and two radiation therapy units for cancer treatment.

Services offered at Heart and Cancer Centre are as following:

Cardiology

  • Cardiology Ambulatory Heart Clinic
  • Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory (Cath Lab)
  • Cardiometabolic Risk (CMR) Clinic
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Open Heart Surgery

​Critical Care

  • Cardio Thoracic Intensive Care Unit (CTICU)
  • Coronary Care Unit (CCU)
  • High Dependency Unit (HDU)
  • High Dependency Unit (HDU)
  • Pediatric High dependency Unit (PHDU)

Oncology

  • Radiation Treatment
  • Medical Oncology / Hematology
  • 3D Mammography
  • Support Services
  • Laboratory Services

Pa​ediatric Specialist Clinics​ ​

  • Newborn clinic​
  • ​Wellbaby clinic
  • Gastroenterology, Hepatology and Nutrition clinic
  • Paediatric haemato-oncology clinic
  • Neonatal clinic​​
